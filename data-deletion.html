How to Request Deletion of Your Data

If you'd like us to delete your account and any campaign data we store, follow one of the methods below. Effective: August 15, 2025.

Summary (quick)

You can request deletion by: 1) using the form below, 2) emailing us directly at [zohaibhussainsathio@gmail.com], or 3) using the authenticated API (for integrators).

What we will delete

  • Your account profile data stored with us (name, email, account metadata).
  • Meta Ads campaign data retrieved on your behalf and stored in our system (campaign IDs, metrics, logs) — unless required to retain for legal obligations.
  • Associated analytics and logs used specifically for your account (we may retain non-identifying aggregates).

How to request deletion

Method A — In-app / interactive (Recommended)

If you are logged into your AI Ads Analyst account, use the form below. We'll verify your ownership (email or OAuth token) and queue the deletion. You will receive confirmation emails during the process.

We verify requests to avoid fraudulent deletion. Provide the email associated with your AI Ads Analyst account and optionally your account ID (if known).

Method B — Email

If you prefer, email the following details to [zohaibhussainsathio@gmail.com] with subject line Data Deletion Request:

Required:
- Email address used to register: your-email@example.com
- Request type: Data Deletion Request

Optional but helpful:
- Account ID (from dashboard)
- Approximate date you last used the app

We will verify your request by sending a confirmation to the registered email address before deleting. If you cannot access that email, we will request additional verification to prevent unauthorized deletion.

Method C — Integrator / API (advanced)

If you are an integrator or developer with an authenticated client, you can call our authenticated API endpoint:

POST https://[[YOUR_DOMAIN]]/api/request-deletion
Headers:
  Authorization: Bearer <your-access-token>
  Content-Type: application/json

Body (JSON):
{
  "email": "you@example.com",
  "account_id": "optional-account-id"
}

Response will include a job ID and estimated completion window. Use the job ID to check status at /api/deletion-status/{jobId}.

What happens after you request deletion

  1. Verification: We verify the request to prevent fraud (email confirmation or OAuth token). This typically completes within 24-72 hours.
  2. Deletion process: Once verified, we queue your account for removal. We will remove active records within 30 days.
  3. Backups: Encrypted backups may persist for a limited retention period per our backup policy; we will schedule removal from backups per our retention schedule and will not use such backups to restore personal data beyond necessary recovery windows.
  4. Confirmation: We will send a final email when deletion completes.

Fraud prevention & verification

To protect accounts from malicious deletion requests we will:

  • Require confirmation via the registered email (or OAuth confirmation).
  • Use rate limiting and identity checks on repeated requests.
  • Request additional proof if the registered email is inaccessible.

If you change your mind or have questions

Contact us at [zohaibhussainsathio@gmail.com] with subject Deletion Question. If deletion is in progress and you request to stop it, we will evaluate based on job state and applicable policy (some deletions are irreversible once started).
