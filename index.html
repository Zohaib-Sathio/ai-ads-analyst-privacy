1. Introduction

AI Ads Analyst ("we", "our", "us") operates a web application that enables users to sign in via Meta (Facebook) OAuth and grant access to campaign data from Meta Ads (Ads Manager). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how you can request deletion.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you authorize our app via Meta OAuth or use the service:

  • From your Meta account: Name and email address (provided via OAuth scope).
  • Meta Ads campaign data: campaign IDs, campaign names, ad sets, ads, and associated performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, spend, CPC, conversions, and related reporting data (retrieved from the Ads API / Ads Manager).
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser and device information, timestamps, and usage logs for analytics and security.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Authentication: To authenticate users via Meta OAuth.
  • Analytics & AI Insight Generation: We analyze your Ads Manager data with our AI system (large language model) to extract insights and generate reports on campaign effectiveness.
  • Query & Reporting: To respond to user queries about their campaigns and to produce downloadable or viewable reports.
  • Service improvement & security: Improve model accuracy, application stability, and detect abuse or unauthorized access. Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for analytics and model improvements.

4. How We Use & Process Your Data

Data is used only to provide the services requested by the user and to operate, secure, and improve the platform. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

  • We may use aggregated, anonymized campaign data to improve AI models. No personal identifiers are included in aggregated datasets.
  • We may share data with trusted subprocessors (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics providers) under contract and confidentiality obligations. These subprocessors only process data as instructed.

5. Data Storage & Security

We store data on secure, encrypted servers. Access to production systems is limited to authorized personnel and protected with multi-factor authentication. You should also ensure your Meta account credentials are protected (strong password, MFA).

6. Data Sharing

We share data only in three cases:

  1. With your explicit consent (for example, if you request a third-party integration or share a report).
  2. With subprocessors performing services for us (hosting, analytics). These parties are bound by contract to protect data.
  3. When required by law or a valid legal process.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal and campaign data while your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 8). We will remove your data from active systems within 30 days after verification of a deletion request; backups may persist for a limited additional time per our backup policy.
